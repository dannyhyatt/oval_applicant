How to play

Guess the president or presidential candidate (soon!) using as few hints as possible. Each hint gets progressively easier and easier. You can also choose to reveal both the party and/or the state that they represent, but this will be reflected in your score. If you do not know the answer after a hint and would not like to hazard a guess, press submit to see the next hint.
